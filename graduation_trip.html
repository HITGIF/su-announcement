2019 Graduation Trip

Graduation is a finish line, it is the finish line of our classmates having classes together, studying together and staying up late together. But the graduation trip is the first and last time that we are traveling together as a group. We always believe that this will be the most unforgettable get together, and to leave all the students with the most memorable memories before going their separate ways in life. The meaning of the graduation travel is quite different from an ordinary travel. Although both are traveling, but the companions are different, and the emotions we have while traveling are also different, so it is different from ordinary travels. This is not only the one and only high school graduation in our lifetime but also, we are striving for the best, and leaving the most beautiful high school memories for everyone.

毕业是一个终点，是我们班级同学一起上课、一起课业，一起熬夜的终点。而毕业旅行是我们高三第一次、也是最后一次集体旅行。我们始终相信，这次毕业旅行会是我们最难忘的一次相聚，为的是给即将各奔前程的同学们留下共同的回忆。毕业旅行和普通旅游意义是截然不同的。虽然都是旅游，但是，同行的人不同，旅游的情感不同，自然是和普通旅游不一样。这是我们仅有一次的高中毕业，我们力求最好，为大家留住美好的高中回忆。

"Tides carves our friendship and the waves record our memories."
「涛声携刻我们友谊，海浪记录我们回忆」
